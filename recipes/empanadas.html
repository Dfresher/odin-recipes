- Empanadas -

Description:

Beef empanadas are a beloved Venezuelan treat that combines a flavorful beef filling with a crispy, golden-brown crust.

These hand-held delights are perfect for snacks, appetizers, or even a meal on the go. Serve them with your favorite dipping sauce for a delightful culinary experience.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup pitted green olives, chopped
  • 1/4 cup raisins, chopped
  • 1/4 cup capers, drained
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 package (about 10) pre-made empanada dough rounds (or make your own dough)

Steps:

  1. Sauté Aromatics:
    • Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    • Add chopped onions and minced garlic. Sauté until onions are translucent.
  2. Brown Ground Beef:
    • Add ground beef to the skillet. Cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
  3. Add Bell Peppers:
    • Stir in diced red and green bell peppers. Cook until the peppers are softened.
  4. Season the Filling:
    • Sprinkle ground cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper over the meat mixture. Mix well to incorporate the spices.
  5. Add Olives, Raisins, and Capers:
    • Include chopped green olives, raisins, and capers. Stir to distribute evenly.
  6. Stir in Tomato Sauce:
    • Pour in the tomato sauce, ensuring all ingredients are coated. Simmer for a few minutes until the mixture thickens.
  7. Prepare Empanadas:
    • Preheat the oven according to the empanada dough package instructions.
    • Place a spoonful of the beef filling in the center of each empanada dough round.
    • Fold the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape. Press the edges to seal, creating a decorative edge if desired.
  8. Bake:
    • Place the empanadas on a baking sheet and bake according to the dough package instructions or until golden brown and crispy.
  9. Serve:
    • Allow the empanadas to cool slightly before serving. Enjoy them on their own or with your favorite dipping sauce.
